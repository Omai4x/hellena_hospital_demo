Who We Are

Built on a Foundation of Care

Hellena Hospital is a fully registered, multi-storey private hospital located at 60B Gas Line, Ohuru Isimiri (before Malting-Plant), Ogbor-Hill, Aba, Abia State, Nigeria. We deliver comprehensive, compassionate, and evidence-based healthcare to every individual who walks through our doors.

Our multi-floor facility is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology — from a fully outfitted operating theatre and neonatal unit to modern inpatient wards and a functioning laboratory. Every space is designed with patient comfort and clinical excellence in mind.

Our mission: To Improve Quality of Life — for patients, their families, and the broader community we serve.

Founder & Medical Director, Hellena Hospital

"Healthcare is not a privilege — it is a right. Hellena Hospital was built on the belief that every person in Aba and Abia State deserves compassionate, world-class medical care, close to home."

Hellena Hospital was born from one person's unwavering conviction that the people of Ogbor-Hill — and all of Aba — deserved better. Our founder, driven by years of medical practice and a deep love for this community, established Hellena Hospital with a singular mission: To Improve Quality of Life.

From a vision conceived in dedication and faith, the hospital grew into a fully equipped, multi-floor medical facility offering surgery, maternity care, paediatrics, diagnostics, and more — all under one roof. Every ward, every piece of equipment, and every member of staff reflects the founder's insistence on excellence without compromise.

Today, Hellena Hospital stands as a testament to that founding spirit — a place where patients are treated as family, and where the community's health is always the highest priority.
