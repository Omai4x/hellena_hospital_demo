Who We Are

Built on a Foundation of Care

Hellena Hospital is a fully registered, multi-storey private hospital located at 60B Gas Line, Ohuru Isimiri (before Malting-Plant), Ogbor-Hill, Aba, Abia State, Nigeria. We deliver comprehensive, compassionate, and evidence-based healthcare to every individual who walks through our doors.

Our multi-floor facility is equipped with state-of-the-art medical technology — from a fully outfitted operating theatre and neonatal unit to modern inpatient wards and a functioning laboratory. Every space is designed with patient comfort and clinical excellence in mind.

Our mission: To Improve Quality of Life — for patients, their families, and the broader community we serve.
