Daily Health Tips

Eat at least 5 portions of fruits and vegetables daily to boost your immune system.

30 minutes of moderate walking daily reduces risk of heart disease and diabetes.

Drink at least 8 glasses of clean water daily. More if pregnant or outdoors.

Adults need 7–9 hours of sleep. Poor sleep weakens immunity and raises blood pressure.

Handwashing with soap prevents up to 80% of common infections.
